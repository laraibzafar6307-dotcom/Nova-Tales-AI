# Nova Tales AI

## Overview

Nova Tales AI is an AI-powered interactive storytelling platform developed to transform traditional novel reading into an immersive and engaging experience.

The platform allows readers to read, listen, interact with AI characters, use voice commands, and provide suggestions directly to the writer. It combines Generative AI, voice technology, and conversational AI to create a modern storytelling ecosystem.

The project is powered by the novel **"Pehchan Ka Sawal"** and demonstrates how artificial intelligence can enhance reader engagement and interactive storytelling.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional novels often provide a passive reading experience with limited interaction between readers and stories.

Modern audiences seek:

- Interactive experiences
- Multimedia storytelling
- AI-assisted engagement
- Real-time character interaction
- Personalized content experiences

Nova Tales AI addresses these challenges by integrating AI technologies directly into the storytelling process.

## Key Features

### Interactive Story Reading
Readers can explore story chapters with scene-based visuals and immersive storytelling.

### Scene-Based Illustrations
Each scene is supported by AI-generated visuals to improve reader engagement.

### Text-to-Speech Narration
Stories can be listened to using natural voice narration.

### Voice Command Support
Users can control reading through voice commands such as:

- Start Reading
- Stop Reading
- Read From This Line

### AI Character Chatbots
Readers can communicate with story characters through real-time conversations.

### Character Calling Feature
Users can initiate voice calls with AI-powered characters.

### Real-Time Conversations
Characters respond intelligently based on story context.

### Suggestion & Feedback Portal
Readers can submit suggestions directly through the platform.

### Direct Writer Feedback System
Feedback is delivered directly to the writer for story improvement.

## Platform Workflow

1. Open Nova Tales AI
2. Choose Reading or Listening Mode
3. Explore Story Chapters

5. Use Voice Commands
6. Submit Suggestions & Feedback
7. Writer Receives Reader Feedback
